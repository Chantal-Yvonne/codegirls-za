FAQ

Everything you need to know

Who can join the program?

Our program is open to all women who are passionate about learning technology and building their careers in tech. No prior coding experience is required - we welcome beginners and those looking to advance their skills.

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior coding experience is necessary! Our program is designed to take you from beginner to proficient. We start with the fundamentals and gradually build up your skills through hands-on projects and mentorship.

How do I apply?

You can apply by filling out the contact form below. Our team will review your application and get back to you with next steps. We accept applications on a rolling basis.
