Changing Lives with CodeGirls

OUR STORY

Our Journey: Empowering Women Through Code

CodeGirls SA was founded with a vision to bridge the gender gap in technology and create opportunities for women to thrive in the digital economy. We believe that education is the key to empowerment, and through our comprehensive training programs, we're helping women build the skills they need to succeed in tech careers.

Since our inception, we've trained hundreds of women in web development, programming, and business skills. Our graduates have gone on to secure positions at leading tech companies, start their own businesses, and become mentors to the next generation of women in tech.

Based in Cape Town, South Africa, we're proud to be part of a global movement that's making technology education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or previous experience.

OUR VALUES

What We Stand For

Empowerment Through Education

We believe that education is the most powerful tool for change. By providing free, high-quality coding education, we're empowering women to take control of their futures and build successful careers in technology.

Inclusive Community

We've created a supportive, inclusive environment where women from all backgrounds can learn, grow, and thrive. Our community is built on mutual respect, collaboration, and the shared goal of success.

Practical Skills

Our curriculum focuses on real-world skills that employers are looking for. From front-end development to full-stack programming, we ensure our students are job-ready from day one.

Lifelong Support

Our commitment doesn't end at graduation. We provide ongoing mentorship, career guidance, and networking opportunities to help our alumni continue growing throughout their careers.
